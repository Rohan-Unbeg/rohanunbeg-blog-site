Frequently Asked Questions

Can I suggest a tool to be reviewed on your blog?

Absolutely! I'm always looking for new tools to review. Please use the contact form with the subject "Tool Suggestion" and include details about the tool and why you think it would be valuable for my readers.

Do you offer advertising opportunities?

Yes, I do offer limited advertising opportunities for relevant developer tools and services. Please contact me using the form with the subject "Advertising" for more information about rates and placement options.

How can I contribute an article to your blog?

I welcome contributions from tech experts! Please email me at contribute@rohanunbeg.com with your article idea, relevant experience, and a brief outline. I'll review your proposal and get back to you promptly.

How do you select tools to review?

I select tools based on several factors: reader requests, relevance to current development trends, potential impact on productivity, and innovation. I aim to provide honest, thorough reviews that help developers make informed decisions.
